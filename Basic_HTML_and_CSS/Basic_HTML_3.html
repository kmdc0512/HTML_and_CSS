Montmartre is primarily known for its artistic history,
the white-domed Basilica of the Sacré-Cœur on its summit,
and as a nightclub district. The other church on the
hill, Saint Pierre de Montmartre, built in 1147, was the
church of the prestigious Montmartre Abbey.
